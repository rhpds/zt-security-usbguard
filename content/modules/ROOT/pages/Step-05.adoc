Monitor USBGuard

Step 5.1 - List the devices seen by USBGuard:
[source,ini,role=execute,subs=attributes+]
----
usbguard list-devices
----

Step 5.2 - Watch live for the USB devices recognized by USBGuard:
[source,ini,role=execute,subs=attributes+]
----
usbguard watch
----
