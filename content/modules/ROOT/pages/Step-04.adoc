Verify USBGuard

Step 4.1 - Verify that the usbguard service is running:
[source,ini,role=execute,subs=attributes+]
----
systemctl status usbguard
----

Step 4.2 - List USB devices recognized by USBGuard:
[source,ini,role=execute,subs=attributes+]
----
usbguard list-devices
----
We have verified that the usbguard service is running, lets go to the next section.
